Introduction

MOHAND MAHDI (Developer Name: NovaMinds) is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the "حساب المعدل-Hisabi Univ Pro" application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information that may be gathered through the application.

1. Information We Collect

Hisabi Univ Pro is designed to help you calculate your academic averages. We collect certain types of information to enhance your experience and provide the app's functionalities.

a. User-Provided Information

Any data you enter into the application (such as grades, weights, subject names) is processed and stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store this data on our servers.

b. Basic Device Information

General, non-identifiable information about your device (such as device type, operating system version, and internet connectivity information) may be collected for the purpose of improving performance and compatibility. This information does not personally identify you.

c. Advertising Information (Currently Inactive - To Be Activated Later)

Important Note: Currently, "Hisabi Univ Pro" does not display ads or collect data for advertising purposes. However, Google AdMob services will be activated to display ads after the closed testing period of the application ends.

Once advertising is activated, Google and its third-party service providers may collect information about your device and app usage (such as unique advertising IDs, device information, IP address, interactions with ads). This is done to personalize ads, improve their effectiveness, and deliver ads relevant to your interests. You can learn more about how Google uses information by visiting Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Operating and Improving App Functionality: To enable you to use app features like calculating averages and saving data locally.
  • Enhancing User Experience: Understanding general app usage to improve design and performance.
  • Ratings and Reviews: We may prompt users to provide ratings and reviews for the app through official app stores.
  • Opening External Links: The app may include links to open related web pages or external resources.
  • Displaying Ads (To Be Activated Later): Once advertising is activated, information collected by Google will be used to deliver targeted and relevant ads to you within the app.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share any personal information or user data that you enter into the application with third parties, as it is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Service Providers (Upon Ad Activation)

Once advertising is activated, non-personal data and advertising IDs will be shared with Google and its advertising service providers for the purpose of displaying targeted ads and measuring their performance. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they process your data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Hisabi Univ Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated Effective Date will become valid upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes, especially when advertising is activated.
